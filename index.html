Immigration Law

We provide free immigration representation to families and victims of violence.

  • U Visa (Victim of certain crimes)
  • T Visa (Victim of human trafficking)
  • VAWA (Battered spouse of USC or LPR)
  • Asylum (Fear of return to home country)
  • SIJS (Unaccompanied minors)
  • DACA (Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals)
  • Family petitions
  • Green Card Renewal
  • Deportation Defense

Family Law

We offer free limited scope family court services for survivors of domestic violence, sexual assault, and human trafficking.

  • Child Custody

  • Child/Spousal Support

Employment Law

We offer free limited scope employment representation and advice to victims of workplace crimes.

  • Retaliation
  • Discrimination
  • Harassment in the Workplace
  • Wrongful Termination
